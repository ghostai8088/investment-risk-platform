- **SR 26-2 (Fed/OCC/FDIC, 2026 — supersedes SR 11-7)** — **VERIFIED** (full text, `sr2602.txt`). The seven materiality passages extracted verbatim; the load-bearing ones: §III *"Model purpose, together with model exposure, determines model materiality"*; §III models of higher materiality *"warrant more comprehensive and rigorous oversight"*; §V *"The nature and rigor of validation generally align with the model's approach, use, and materiality"*; §V monitoring *"frequency and scope … will depend on … model materiality"*. **Inherent risk is defined separately** (assumptions, *"the model's complexity"*, input quality) — ⚠️ **complexity is NOT part of SR 26-2 materiality**; a composite tier must not be labeled with it (OD-A's split exists because of this sentence).
