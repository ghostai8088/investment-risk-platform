## Part 2 — Decision detail (the non-obvious ones)

### OD-P3-3-A/B/C — why allocation over pinned exposure atoms is the honest v1
A factor exposure can be (i) a **vendor-supplied loading** (β from Barra/Axioma-style models) × position value — but **no factor-loading entity has ever been captured** (P3-2 captured returns + definitions only); capturing loadings is a P2-style captured-input slice, and building it INSIDE P3-3 would fuse the captured-input and derived-number patterns in one slice (the exact confusion the two-pattern rule exists to prevent). Or (ii) a **regression-estimated loading** — needs instrument/portfolio **return history** (gated on adjusted/total-return prices, an OD-P3-0-K named gap) plus estimation-window/decay machinery that is P3-4's covariance substrate; pulling it into P3-3 is the scope-creep P3-0 explicitly fenced. Or (iii) a **membership/indicator exposure** — the form fundamental factor models actually use for currency/industry/country blocks (the loading IS membership). Only (iii) stands entirely on shipped, governed inputs: the `exposure_aggregate` atom (the platform's governed market value of a position, IA-immutable ⇒ perfectly pinnable, carrying its own `mark_currency`) and the P3-2 `factor` definitions (the factor universe with a captured `currency_code` scope). So v1 = indicator-loading currency-factor exposure: genuine factor-model output, exact sum-to-total, zero new captured data, and the FULL OD-P3-0-F contract exercised — while (i) and (ii) are recorded as named prerequisites, not silently absorbed. The roadmap's "consumes factor returns + positions" is treated exactly as OD-P3-1-A treated "curves + positions": aspirational framing refined by grounding — P3-3 consumes the P3-2 factor **universe**; the P3-2 **returns** become load-bearing at P3-4 (their designed first consumer) and at regression v2.

### OD-P3-3-B — why atoms, not re-rolled positions
Consuming positions+valuations+FX directly (the `PURPOSE_EXPOSURE_INPUT` build path) would force `run_factor_exposure` to re-implement `signed_quantity × mark × FX` inside the risk engine — a **second compute path for the same governed number**, which can drift from `run_exposure` and makes the factor exposure disagree with the exposure report built from the same inputs. Consuming the **completed exposure run's IA rows** gives one source of truth for market value, a stronger lineage story (`factor_exposure` transitively depends on the exposure run's own snapshot via the pinned atoms), and the strongest possible pin (immutable rows cannot drift — `content_hash` verification is exact by construction). The consumed exposure run must be `COMPLETED` (a FAILED run has zero rows ⇒ pre-create refusal), own-tenant, `run_type='EXPOSURE_AGGREGATE'`.

### OD-P3-3-D — ENT-028 family, not a net-new mint
ENT-028 is reserved as **`sensitivity` / `exposure_metric`** ("Greeks/duration/PFE/etc."). A factor exposure is definitionally an exposure metric; minting a net-new canonical id would duplicate an existing reservation's semantic space (the Data-Architecture lens's ENT-030 lesson at P3-0 — check the reserved inventory before minting). The canonical model bookkeeps `factor_exposure_result` as a second ENT-028 realization beside `sensitivity_result` (the ENT-009 header+detail and ENT-021/023-by-value precedents show one canonical id carrying multiple realizations cleanly). The alternative (net-new `factor_exposure` id) is recorded and available if a future factor-analytics block outgrows the family — a documentation move, not a schema one.

### OD-P3-3-G — why this is a model, not "just bucketing arithmetic"
The P2-3 `code_version`-only exemption covers a **convention-free** transform (`Σ qty × mark × FX` has no methodology choice). The allocation exposure embeds real methodology choices: the family→attribute correspondence (currency exposure defined by **mark currency** — a declared proxy for denomination currency, with its limitation recorded), indicator (not fractional) loadings, exact-match semantics, and the partition-or-fail rule. Different reasonable choices give different numbers ⇒ it is a model under OD-P3-0-E, binds a registered `model_version`, and carries a methodology doc — which also makes CTRL-003's inventory gate load-bearing for the second risk number (the contract generalizes beyond its P3-1 debut).

### OD-P3-3-I — the IA pin flavor (new, but schema-free)
`dataset_snapshot_component` was designed with nullable pin columns; FR pins use `(valid_from, system_from, record_version)`, EV pins `(NULL, NULL, record_version)`. The exposure atom introduces the third flavor — IA: `(NULL, system_from, NULL)` + `captured_content`/`content_hash`. No table change; `_reresolve_content` re-reads the immutable row by id and re-hashes (always byte-identical unless tampered — which is exactly what verification exists to catch). One component per atom (the POSITION/VALUATION per-row precedent; there is no immutable set-header entity to hang a set-grained pin on — the status-mutable `calculation_run` is deliberately not content-pinned).
