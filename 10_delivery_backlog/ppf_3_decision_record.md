## Part 0 — What PPF-3 actually is: a REPARTITION, not a naive addition (the verifier's core correction)

The first draft framed PPF-3 as "add `p'Ω_pp·p` to PA-4's *unchanged* `σ_total`." **Both verifiers REFUTED that as a double-count** and they are right (Part 5). The corrected design:

- **PA-4's residual leg is the WHOLE non-public residual.** `σ_e,i` = the RS-1 residual stdev of instrument `i`'s desmoothed return regressed on its promoted public proxy (`Var(desmoothed − w·R_public)`). PPF-1's pure-private return is the SAME residual series: `pp_i = desmoothed − Σ_promoted w·R = e_i + b_0 + Σ_dropped b·R`, so `Var(pp_i) ⊇ σ_e,i²`. Adding `p'Ω_pp·p` (leg 2) while keeping PA-4's residual (leg 3) counts each private-segment member's non-public variance **twice**. On the single-member demo segment it is a ~100% double-count.
- **The fix (OD-PPF-3-G): repartition.** A proxied instrument that is a MANUAL member of a pure-private segment carried in `p`/Ω_pp has its non-public variance counted in **leg 2 (the Ω_pp block) ONLY** and is **REMOVED from leg 3**. A proxied instrument with NO pure-private segment keeps its PA-4 diagonal residual in leg 3. Every instrument's non-public variance is in **exactly one** leg.
- **The value over PA-4's total VaR is therefore precisely the OFF-DIAGONAL of Ω_pp.** For the private-segment members, leg 2's full Ω_pp block REPLACES leg 3's independent diagonal: `σ²_unified − σ²_total = 2·Σ_{s<t} p_s·p_t·Ω_pp[s,t]/d_t + (small diagonal estimator difference)`. **The unified number captures that a portfolio's private books CO-MOVE in their pure-private risk — which total VaR (independent diagonals) misses.** For a SINGLE private asset there is no off-diagonal, so `σ_unified ≈ σ_total` (a coherence property — it should reduce). ⇒ the headline is only non-trivial with **≥2 private segments** (OQ-3-3 = B).
- **What survives from the "assembly" framing:** legs 1 (`x'Σx`) and the residual-over-the-rest reuse PA-4 verbatim; the genuinely new math is the private-block quadratic form `p'(Ω_pp/d_t)·p` + the repartition filter on leg 3. The frequency conversion is NOT a new mint (PA-4's `d_t = appraisal_days·252/365` is reused; the whole Ω_pp matrix de-scales by `1/d_t`).

## Part 2 — Rule-6 external benchmark grounding (fetch/cite RAN 2026-07-22)

- **√-time scaling of a DESMOOTHED covariance — DEFENSIBLE, contingent on i.i.d.** Desmoothing (Getmansky-Lo-Makarov 2004; Geltner 1993; Okunev-White 2003) strips the MA autocorrelation to recover an ≈i.i.d. true series; under i.i.d., variance ∝ time, so `Ω_pp/d_t` is the internally-consistent de-scale (the covariance analog of PA-4's `σ_e/√d_t` — verifier 1 confirmed both are the variance de-scale). Caveat (Danielsson-Zigrand 2006): √-time is exactly valid only under i.i.d. and transports the SECOND MOMENT cleanly; VaR **tail**-scaling degrades under jumps/fat tails. Recorded control (v2): a residual-ACF diagnostic (Ljung-Box) on `pp`; Marcato-Key 2007 (≤2 lags typically suffice).
- **Block-diagonal (public↔private) Ω_pp — DEFENSIBLE first cut, EXTRA-justified.** `pp` is a public-*residual* by construction, so the public↔pure-private cross-covariance is ≈0 by orthogonalization; the PPF-2 refinement stands (promoted-weights SUBSET → a small, **sign-ambiguous** residual cross-term). Vendor state-of-the-art (Shepard 2015, MSCI Barra Integrated Model) = a **global-factor linkage** = the named v2, NOT block-diagonal. **NB (verifier): this disposition is about leg1↔leg2 and does NOT address the leg2↔leg3 double-count — a separate axis, fixed by OD-3-G.**
- **Leverage — a SEPARABLE outer multiplier; deferring to v2 is defensible.** MSCI factorizes leverage as an outer `×Leverage` and uses *relative/reported* leverage, not full look-through; ILPA requires unlevered-and-levered dual reporting. ⇒ v1 = an honest **"unlevered / at-average-leverage systematic VaR"** (leverage=1.0), labeled so (OD-3-E). v2 = MSCI relative-leverage.
- **Regulatory — no unified-VaR mandate.** ILPA (leverage transparency) + Basel FRTB d457 (liquidity-horizon scaling, look-through leverage hierarchy) support the architecture, don't constrain it.

### OD-PPF-3-G — The REPARTITION: no leg2↔leg3 double-count (the verifier's blocking fold)

Separately estimating `a_i = pp_i − pp_s` (so leg 3 carries `Var(a_i)` for multi-member segments) is the recorded **v2**. **Coherence property (a guardrail, not a bug):** for a portfolio with a SINGLE private asset, leg 2 has no off-diagonal, so `σ_unified ≈ σ_total` (it MUST reduce). Regression tests assert both this reduction and the two-segment identity `σ²_unified − σ²_total = 2·MV_PE·MV_PC·Ω_pp[PE,PC]/d_t` (both fail under the naive additive formula — the anti-double-count guardrails).
