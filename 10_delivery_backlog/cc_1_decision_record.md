### OD-A ‚Äî Commitment grain: (portfolio_id, instrument_id) ‚Äî the committing portfolio √ó the private-fund INSTRUMENT (Tier-3, OQ-1)

ENT-015 `commitment` binds **`portfolio_id` (the tenant book whose unfunded exposure it is) + `instrument_id` (the private fund committed to ‚Äî an ordinary `instrument` row, `asset_class ‚àà PRIVATE_*`, the PA-0 convention)**, mirroring position/valuation/transaction's two-FK shape.
